Benefits of Condoms

A supply of various brands and types of condoms will be provided within each vending machine and dispenser.

  • Prevents the increasing spread of STDs.

  • Prevents accidental pregnancy.

  • Prevents the spread of human papillomavirus (HPV).

  • Reduce the risk of a UTI.

  • Easy to use and safe.

  • Cost effective and efficient.

Benefits of Levonorgestrel

A progestin — which can prevent ovulation, block fertilization or keep a fertilized egg from implanting in the uterus. Prevents pregnancy after unprotected sex or after another birth control method fails.

  • Prevents accidental pregancy.

  • Cost effective solution to accidental pregnancy.

  • Suitable for the treatment of menorrhagia.

  • 95% effective if taken within 24 hours

  • 89% effective if taken within 72 hours

Benefits of Feminine Hygiene

Feminine hygiene products are personal care products used during menstruation, vaginal discharge, and other bodily functions related to the vulva and vagina.

  • Provides access to essential products for women.

  • Encourages positive hygiene.

  • Provided for the cheapest price in the neighborhood.

Over The Counter Medication

We will provide over the counter (OTC) medication products. This will allow students and residents to easily access non prescription medications. OTC medications are used for symptomatic relief of minor health conditions, including headaches, coughs, colds, allergies, fever and more.

  • Easily Accessible

  • Cost much less than prescription medications.

  • Saves more than 42% a month according to bluecrossnc.com

  • No lines & no hassle







Why We Do It

Prevention

STD rates are currently at an all time high. By providing products for safer sex we hope to help slow down and prevent the spread of STD's throughout our community.

Health

We want to protect our women’s health and make them feel more comfortable within our community by providing what is necessary to keep up with good feminine hygiene.

Emergency Contraception

It is important to provide emergency contraceptives for the times when protection fails. We will always make sure our locations always have them emergecy contraceptives available.
