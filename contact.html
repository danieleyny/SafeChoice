Some common questions
we are often asked

What is the purpose of the vending machines?

The pharmacy vending machines were implemented as an initiative to provide students and residents with 24/7 access to affordable over-the-counter (OTC) medications, contraceptives, and personal care products.

Where are the vending machines located?

The vending machines are placed at the entrance of campus dorms and residential buildings making them accessible at all times.

What products are available in the vending machines?

Products are subject to change as we grow. As of now we provide Condoms, Levonorgestrel products, Tampons, Pads, Pain & Fever Medication, Allergy & Cold Medication, Pregnancy tests and Personal Hygiene Products (Deodorant, Lip balm, Tissues, Wipes, and Hand Sanitizer).

Where do the products in the vending machines come from?

Our products are sourced from New Yorks largest buisness wholesales along with certified pharmacies.

How does the vending machine product pricing work?

Before installing our vending machines we analyze the zip codes average price for every product and aim to always provide the most cost effective products in the area.

How can we have products added?

To request for a product to be added please reach out to us through our contact section. Let us know which product you'd like to see in your local vending machine and we will work with you to make it happen!
